Litigation

Lawsuits
I represent plaintiffs and defendants in civil lawsuits. I manage all phases of the litigation from the investigation, pleadings, and discovery through the pre-trial, trial, settlement, and appeal processes.
Dispute Resolution
I will advise my client on their legal position, and what chance he/she has of winning the case at court or in an arbitration.As the trial or hearing nears, I might brief a barrister to represent my client, or prepare to argue my client's case myself.
Finance and
Accounting
I can arrange loans to buy other companies, or to finance new business ventures. I also give legal advice, and work on a variety of corporate and finance-related cases.
Appeals
I handle cases on appeal when a party loses or is unhappy with some part of the decision made by the lower court. The appeals court reviews the record made in the trial court and decides whether to affirm or to reverse the trial court's decision based upon the written briefs.
Federal
I work with all three branches of government in numerous capacities, including litigating civil and criminal cases, counselling lawmakers, drafting statutes and regulations, issuing administrative legal opinions, and much more.

Business Law

Outside general
counsel services
Guidelines for in-house counsel to provide to outside counsel setting out the policies and procedures governing their relationship, including legal fees, billing and expense guidelines, conflicts of interest, budgeting, and preserving confidentiality and privilege.
Startup and
Formation
Working as a startup lawyer I become an essential partner to a new business. I can prevent legal problems from hurting your business down the line and help you before you face a costly lawsuit or troubling legal situation.

Family Law

Divorce
I’m responsible for the division of assets and debt among spouses. If there are children involved, a I help to set the terms for child custody and child support. For legal separations, I carry out the separation through court orders.
Custody
I will advocate for my client and help create a custody agreement that is in the child's best interest. If the parents cannot come to an agreement, I petition the court for custody on the client's behalf.
Child Support
I work to protect the rights of minors in cases involving divorce, child custody, neglect or abuse, and juvenile court proceedings. Courts will appoint me in the following circumstances: Neglect or abuse of the minor.
Alimony
My goal is to limit any unfair economic effects of a divorce by providing a continuing income to a non-wage-earning or lower-wage-earning spouse.I also can help a spouse continue the standard of living they had during marriage.
