Meet the attorney

Jeff Y. Linn

Jeff Lin was previously an attorney with the downtown Minneapolis law firm of Moss & Barnett, P.A., where he specialized in the complex financial aspects of electric utility rate cases, notably for client Xcel Energy. His work experience include capital structure and return on equity (ROE), accounting treatment and cost recovery of expenses, tax treatment of stand alone versus consolidated tax structures, and numerous regulatory filings.

In his solo practice, Jeff specialized in civil litigation, having case experience in lawsuits for breach of contract, breach of fiduciary duty, defamation, divorce, and probate litigation.

Jeff has the range of experience to advise individuals and companies, both large and small, on their legal strategy, and the available legal options to achieve their goals.

Jeff brings a personal touch to his legal practice these days, preferring to work with clients who want a dedicated attorney who will find creative solutions to their legal matters.

Prior to his legal work, Jeff was also a tax accountant with Arthur Andersen, an audit accountant with PricewaterhouseCoopers, a financial analyst with startup Metamarkets.com, and a senior financial analyst with Oracle Corporation.

Jeff is an avid traveler and photographer. He has traveled to over 53 countries, including most of Europe and Asia.

Jeff speaks fluent English and Mandarin Chinese, as well as conversational Russian.
